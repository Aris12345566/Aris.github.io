December

December is a Neck Deep song released in 2015 in the album titled Life's Not Out to Get You. The meaning of the December song is about a man who is heartbroken in December. The man felt his heart was broken after his lover cheated on him with another man.

In Bloom

“In Bloom” by Neck Deep delves into themes of mental struggles and personal growth. This poignant song captures the essence of battling one’s own mind and the journey to wellness. It reflects the feelings of frustration and the desire for understanding and improvement.
